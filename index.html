About Us

Law Bridge Zambia is a student-led initiative in Lusaka, connecting law students with practicing professionals through mentorship, skills workshops, and helping the community learn about Law.

What We Offer

Mentorship & Networking

One-on-one guidance, panel discussions, and virtual meetups with legal experts.

Skills Workshops

Hands-on training in legal research, drafting, mooting, and courtroom etiquette.

Career Opportunities

Access internships, clerkships, and volunteer roles at leading law firms and NGOs.

Online Community

Join our social channels for legal updates, study resources, and event announcements.

Meet Our Team

Our dedicated team of legal students and experts are committed to bridging the gap between legal education and practice in Zambia.
